The WhatsApp integration lets you communicate with customers through WhatsApp directly from SmartPyme, without switching between apps. You can manage conversations, track session status, and view activity summaries all from a single panel.

## Activating WhatsApp

The WhatsApp module is an optional add-on that must be enabled for your SmartPyme account before you can use it. Contact your SmartPyme administrator to request activation. Once enabled, the **WhatsApp** option appears in the left sidebar.

## Connecting a WhatsApp Session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp panel">
    Click **WhatsApp** in the left sidebar to open the WhatsApp management area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new session">
    Click **Connect Session**. SmartPyme generates a QR code that you will use to link your WhatsApp Business account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Open the **WhatsApp Business** app on your phone, go to **Linked Devices**, and tap **Link a Device**. Point your camera at the QR code displayed in SmartPyme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    After a successful scan, the session status updates to **Connected**. Your WhatsApp Business number is now linked to SmartPyme.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sending Messages

Once a session is connected, use the **Messaging** panel to send and receive WhatsApp messages with your customers. Select a contact or enter a phone number to start a conversation. All messages are logged within SmartPyme so your team maintains a shared view of every customer interaction.

## Managing Sessions

The **WhatsApp Sessions** panel gives you a full view of every linked number. From there you can:

- **View** which sessions are currently active or inactive.
- **Disconnect** a session to unlink a number from SmartPyme.
- **Reconnect** a session by scanning a new QR code if a connection drops.

Each session represents one linked WhatsApp Business number. You can maintain multiple sessions if your business operates more than one number.

## Executive Summary

The **Executive Summary** tab inside the WhatsApp panel provides an overview of your messaging activity, including the number of conversations opened, messages sent, and response metrics over a selected date range. Use this data to monitor your team's responsiveness and customer communication volume.

<Warning>
  Only connect **WhatsApp Business** accounts to SmartPyme. Personal WhatsApp accounts are not designed for business use and may be blocked or banned by Meta when used for commercial messaging.
</Warning>
