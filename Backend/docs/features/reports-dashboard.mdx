SmartPyme's analytics dashboard gives you a live pulse on your business without opening a spreadsheet. Key financial and operational metrics update in real time as your team records sales, expenses, and inventory movements — so you always have an accurate, up-to-date picture of how your business is performing.

## Dashboard Overview

Access the main dashboard by clicking **Dashboard** in the left navigation menu. At a glance you will see:

- **Total sales** for the selected period.
- **Cash flow** across your registered accounts.
- **Top-selling products** by revenue or quantity.
- **Expense breakdown** by category.
- **Inventory value** of your current stock.

## Date Filters

Use the **date picker** at the top of the dashboard to define any custom date range — today, this month, a fiscal quarter, or a specific span of days. You can also filter results by **branch** or **sales channel** to isolate performance for a specific location or revenue stream.

## Automatic Reports (Reportes Automáticos)

You can schedule SmartPyme to email reports to your team on a recurring basis. To configure automatic reports:

1. Go to **Settings → Automatic Reports**.
2. Click **New Automatic Report**.
3. Choose the **report type** (sales, expenses, inventory, etc.).
4. Set the **frequency**: daily, weekly, or monthly.
5. Add the **recipients** who should receive the report by email.

SmartPyme will generate and deliver the report on schedule, even when no one is logged in.

## Exporting Data

Almost every list view in SmartPyme includes an **Export** button. Use it to download the current view's data for further analysis or record-keeping. The following modules support Excel and PDF exports:

- **Sales** — full transaction history with line items.
- **Inventory** — stock levels, movements, and valuations.
- **Payroll** — payslips, payroll runs, and employee summaries.
- **Accounting** — journal entries, ledgers, and financial statements.

<Tip>
  Combine dashboard sections with the **branch filter** to compare performance side by side across all your locations — ideal for multi-branch businesses that need to identify which sites are driving growth.
</Tip>
